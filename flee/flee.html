Flee

How To Play

Game controls:

Mouse: move the square
Space: start/stop
N: new game

Rules of the game:


  1. The balls speed up a little every 5 seconds.
  2. Powers are provided randomly every 10 seconds and will be available to be collected for only 2 seconds. The powers are to be collected by moving the square over them.
    • : player attains invincibility for 5 seconds i.e. the game continues even if contact is made with the balls. The square turns green for the time being.
    • : the square shrinks to half its size for 8 seconds.
  3. The game can be paused anytime during gameplay using the space bar but player loses power effectivity time during the paused time.
